📌 About This App

📌 Smart Task Management

Easily create, update, and track your to-do items with real-time status and deadlines.

⏰ Deadline Alerts

Get notified when tasks are overdue or close to their deadline to stay on track.

📊 Stats & History

View performance insights and task history to improve your productivity over time.

🔗 Share & Collaborate

Share your task lists with friends, teammates, or clients via a simple link. Guest and host modes ensure privacy and control.

🔒 Secure Data Sync

Your tasks are safely stored in Firebase with real-time syncing. Access them anytime, anywhere, on any device.
